The youth turnout gap that started my capstone

Turnout in the 2019 European elections in France, by age group.

27%
40%
49%
55%

67%
18-2425-3435-4950-5960-6465+

The pattern is hard to miss: turnout climbs with every age bracket, from 27% among 18-24s to 67% among over-65s. Young people were not just quieter at the ballot box, they were absent from it. That gap is what my team built Attention Élections! to close, a two-part intervention to lower the start-up cost of voting for university students. We measured an 11% rise in election awareness pre to post.

Source: IFOP, 2019 European election turnout. Analysis and intervention from my Sciences Po capstone. Chart built for the web.
